About

Cera Brasileira is the Brazilian Hard Wax pioneer in Indonesia and was introduced in 2017. We are the first and only original Brazilian Hard Wax, using a traditional recipe passed down from generation to generation in Brazil since 1992. Made from natural ingredients, containing propolis with antibacterial properties and enriched with pure honey to moisturize your skin. Suitable for both women and men, and safe for pregnant and breastfeeding mothers. Can be used on the entire face, underarms, chest, intimate areas, hands, and feet.
